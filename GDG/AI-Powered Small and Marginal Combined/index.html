How KrishiSathi Works

Our simple four-step process helps you implement smart farming techniques without complexity.

1

Register Your Farm

Sign up for KrishiSathi and provide basic details about your farm location, size, and current crops. You can register through our mobile app, website, or with the help of a local KrishiSathi representative.

2

Connect Soil Sensors (Optional)

For enhanced precision, you can install our affordable IoT soil sensors that measure moisture levels, temperature, and nutrient content. Don't worry‚Äîour system works even without sensors by using satellite data.

3

Receive Personalized Recommendations

Our AI analyzes your farm's data along with climate patterns, soil conditions, and market trends to provide you with personalized recommendations for crop selection, irrigation scheduling, fertilizer application, and disease prevention.

4

Implement and Track Progress

Follow the recommendations and track your farm's progress through our easy-to-use dashboard. The system continuously learns from your feedback and results to improve future recommendations.
